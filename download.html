Download for iOS

Install Together directly from the official App Store.

  1. If you are on your iPhone or iPad, tap this link to open Together on the App Store: Together: Daily To-Do Planner
  2. Tap “Get” and follow the usual App Store steps to complete the installation.

If you are browsing from another device, open the App Store on your iPhone and search for “Together: Daily To-Do Planner”.

Download for Android

To install our app on your Android device:

  1. Click this link to download the APK directly: Download APK
  2. You may need to allow installs from unknown sources in your device settings.
  3. Once the file is downloaded, open the APK to start the installation.
